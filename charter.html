Postgres-X2 Development Group Charter

Activity

The Posgres-X2 Development Group performs any activities to develop, publish, support and any other activities to help them related to Postgres-X2 core features. Postgres-X2 is a database cluster based upon PostgreSQL database.

Members

Core Members

As of May 1st, 2015, Core Team consists of the following people (alphabetical order):

  • Koichi Suzuki
  • Lee Gaylee

Core members appoints its own members.

Committers

As of May 1st, 2015, the following people are committers (alphabetical order):

  • Abbas Butt
  • Koichi Suzuki
  • Galy Lee

The core team assigns committers.`

Development members

Development members consist of those who contribute the code, report bugs, test the core features, propose new features, discuss about Postgres-X2 and related feature through Postgres-X2 mailing lists, or those who provide any other activities which helps Postgres-X2 development.

Committee

Postgres-X2 development group may have committees to carry out its activities. As of May 1st, 2015, there is no such committees.

Representative

Core team chooses its representative. As of May 1st, 2015, the representative is Koichi Suzuki.

Location

Postgres-X2 Development Groups location is that of the representative.

Governing Law

Postgres-X2 development Group will be governed by laws at its location.
